Gr8Builds is an architecture designed for infinite scale, not a collection of tools.

The unified data layer makes our Artificial Intelligence orders of magnitude more effective.
* **Competitors:** Their Artificial Intelligence is "data-starved" because it can only access data trapped in one silo.
