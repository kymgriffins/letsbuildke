Below is a **complete, structured, non-generic automation and AI blueprint** for a **modern construction company**. It covers **end-to-end operations**, **site workflows**, **financials**, **procurement**, **HSE**, **HR**, **engineering**, **client services**, **sales**, and **executive intelligence**.
This is the “full stack” used globally by top-tier construction firms.

---

# 1. EXECUTIVE & COMPANY-LEVEL AUTOMATIONS

## 1.1. Strategic Intelligence

* AI-powered executive dashboard (real-time cost, schedule, risk, cashflow).
* Predictive project overruns (AI model forecasts delays + cost blowouts).
* Portfolio-level resource forecasting (equipment, crews, subcontractors).
* Contract risk scoring on tenders.
* Automated competitor analysis and market intelligence.

* Automated contract generation from templates.
